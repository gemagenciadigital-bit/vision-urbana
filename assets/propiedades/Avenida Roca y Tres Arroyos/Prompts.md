# Librería de Prompts Cinematográficos - Proyecto Premium Hub (Visión Urbana)

Esta librería contiene prompts optimizados estructuralmente con técnicas de cinematografía profesional (Drone, POV, Gimbal) y un enfoque **Biofílico/Realista** para una secuencia de video fluida en CapCut.

## 1. Salón Comercial (Interior Frontal)
### Imagen Estática (Atardecer Biofílico)
> **Prompt:** "Interior architectural photography of a premium 4x4 showroom at Golden Hour. Polished light-grey concrete floors reflecting warm sunset light. Black structural columns integrated with vertical warm LED strips and natural climbing ivy. Large glass windows showing a vibrant orange sky. A rugged 4x4 Ford Raptor is the centerpiece. Abundant indoor plants in large clay pots creating a natural atmosphere. Soft, airy lighting, professional studio fill lights, 8k resolution."

### Video Transicional (Gimbal Reveal)
> **Prompt:** "Cinematic Gimbal shot transitioning from an empty commercial space to a lush 4x4 showroom. The camera moves forward (Push-in) steadily. Original tiles morph into polished concrete as lush greenery and tropical plants grow into the frame. The lighting shifts dynamically from flat daylight to a warm, glowing sunset. Warm LED strips on columns flicker on. High-quality morphing, atmospheric nature textures, photorealistic."

### Paneo General (POV Original)
> **Prompt:** "Smooth POV walking shot of the original empty space. Handheld stabilized camera (Gimbal) moving at eye level. Captures the raw state of the black columns and tiled floor without any modifications. Natural daylight, realistic documentary style."

## 2. Gran Galpón (Interior Trasero)
### Imagen Estática (Lounge & Workshop Realista)
> **Prompt:** "Modern industrial lounge and workshop inside a large warehouse. Charcoal-grey brick walls, exposed metal trusses with warm festoon lights. Comfortable tan leather sofas and a low wooden coffee table with small potted plants. In the background, a clean glass-walled workshop. Soft natural light from skylights mixed with warm interior accents. Professional architectural visualization, high-quality textures."

### Video Transicional (Drone Interior Fly-through)
> **Prompt:** "FPV Drone fly-through transition. The camera glides through the industrial warehouse at a medium height. The space morphs from a dusty empty shell into a warm, inviting VIP lounge. Furniture and decorations appear as if being built in fast-motion. Plants and wooden textures wrap around structural elements. Smooth lighting transition to a cozy evening glow. Fluid motion, cinematic 4k."

### Paneo General (Drone Pan)
> **Prompt:** "Slow aerial drone pan shot of the original industrial warehouse. Wide-angle lens capturing the full scale of the brick walls and corrugated roof. No architectural changes, maintaining original textures and overhead natural light."

## 3. Fachada Exterior
### Imagen Estática (Atardecer Premium)
> **Prompt:** "Premium commercial facade at Golden Hour. Matte black architectural cladding with realistic sunset reflections. Slim black aluminum frames with warm interior lights visible. Dramatic orange and deep blue sunset sky. Ground up-lighting in amber tones. Clean sidewalk with small integrated planters and green bushes. Luxury real estate photography style, high contrast."

### Video Transicional (Drone Orbit Reveal)
> **Prompt:** "Cinematic Drone Orbit transition. The camera circles around the building facade. The original weathered exterior morphs into the new matte black cladding as the sun sets in the background. Windows clear up and lights inside the showroom turn on one by one. Smooth transition between day and night, breathtaking sunset colors, ultra-realistic."

### Paneo General (Drone Sideways Tracking)
> **Prompt:** "Professional drone tracking shot moving parallel to the original facade. Steady horizontal movement at mid-level. Captures the original black paint, windows, and sidewalk in their current state. Natural daylight, high-quality original footage style."

## 5. Patio de Exhibición Biofílico (Entrega)
### Imagen Estática (Oasis de Entrega)
> **Prompt:** "Realistic biophilic vehicle handover area at sunset. Light-grey polished microcement floor. Columns covered in natural greenery and climbing vines. Large stone planters with tropical foliage. Warm festoon lights hanging under the metal roof. Back walls with natural wood slats. Soft, warm golden sunset light filtering through the plants. A premium 4x4 parked naturally in the center. Welcoming and organic atmosphere."

### Video Transicional (Crane Down Slide)
> **Prompt:** "Cinematic Crane-down transition shot. The camera slides down from the metal roof towards the floor. The empty patio morphs into a lush biophilic oasis. Plants grow into the scene, wood panels appear on walls, and warm party lights turn on. The lighting transitions beautifully to a deep sunset. A 4x4 vehicle is revealed as the camera reaches eye level. High-fidelity natural textures."

### Paneo General (Stabilized Gimbal Pan)
> **Prompt:** "Slow, stabilized gimbal pan shot of the original outdoor patio. Captures the rough concrete and metal structure in a smooth movement. No digital additions, realistic architectural survey style."
